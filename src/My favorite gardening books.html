My favorite gardening books

Posted by Jalini Jose on May 16, 2023

I have selected my favourite and most used books to help you on your journey


1. 'RHS How to Garden' series by the Royal Horticultural Society (RHS) -

These are a series of practical guidebooks that cover a wide range of gardening topics, from containers to fruits and vegetables to ornamentals.


2. 'The Well-Tempered Garden' by Christopher Lloyd -

This classic book is packed with beautiful illustrations and offers a unique approach to garden design.


3. 'The Flower Garden: How to Create the Perfect Garden' by Tessa Evelegh -

This book provides expert advice on flower gardening, from planting and pruning to choosing the right plants for your soil type.


4. 'The Complete Vegetable Gardener' by Monty Don -

This comprehensive guide covers everything you need to know about growing vegetables, from seed starting to harvesting.


5. 'The New Organic Grower' by Eliot Coleman -

This book is a great resource for those interested in organic gardening and sustainable farming practices.
